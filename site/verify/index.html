What this checks — and what it does not

  • Checks here, in your browser: each photo is unchanged since capture (content-hash fixity), the producer's digital signature over the record, and the append-only chain of custody.
  • Not checked here: the full RFC 3161 trusted-timestamp certificate chain. Timestamps are shown as present; to confirm them cryptographically, run habitable verify or use standard RFC 3161 / OpenSSL tooling.
  • This page never proves who took a photo, that it depicts a particular home, the underlying condition, or admissibility. It proves the record was not altered after the fact.
