habitable

Verify a habitability evidence packet

No install, no upload: choose a packet and this page re-checks its content hashes, trusted timestamps, chain of custody, and producer signature — entirely in your browser.

Alpha software. This is a working reference implementation; do not rely on it for real legal matters yet.

Nothing leaves your device

Everything on this page happens in your browser, on your device. The files you choose are never uploaded — there is no server behind this page and it makes no network requests. Once the page is open (or saved), it works with no internet connection.

Choose the packet

A packet is a folder containing bundle.json, bundle.sig.json, and a media/ folder (and sometimes originals/). Choose the whole folder so every file can be checked.

Drag and drop the packet folder (or its files) here, or use the pickers below.

    How it verifies
    
        
    
          This page is a JavaScript port of habitable's standalone verifier, pinned to the
          verifier
          decision table and cross-tested against the Python verifier in CI. It fails closed:
